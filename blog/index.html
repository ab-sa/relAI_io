Dr Abdollah Safari is a statistician at the Faculty of Applied Sciences with an interest almost in every application of statistics. He has been involved in different types of projects in a wide range of areas from bioinformatics and biostatistics to marketing and advertisement, and collaborating with researchers from diverse educational backgrounds in academia and industry. In his current role with the Faculty, he heads the GrnTAZ Statistical Consulting company. Dr. Safari received his PhD in statistics from Simon Fraser University in 2017, where he focused mainly on generalized linear mixed models and survival analysis on Big Data problems.
